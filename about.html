About STILLETO Restaurant

Our Story

STILLETO Restaurant was established in 2010, as a result of its passion for providing exceptional dining experiences. What started as a small family-owned restaurant has since evolved into a beloved culinary destination known for its innovative cuisine and warm hospitality.

Mission and Values

Our mission is simple: to delight our guests with great flavors, impeccable service and a welcoming atmosphere. We are committed to using the finest locally sourced ingredients, promoting sustainability, and supporting our community.

Our Chef

Chef Mohamed Ammar, a culinary visionary with over 10 years of experience, leads our kitchen.

Cuisine

At STILLETO, we specialize in contemporary Palestinian cuisine with an emphasis on seasonal ingredients. Our menu features a variety of dishes, from artisanal small plates to mouth-watering main courses, all prepared with precision and care.

Restaurant Ambiance

Our restaurant offers an elegant and relaxed atmosphere. With its comfortable seating, warm lighting, and soothing music, it is the perfect place for romantic dinners, family celebrations, and happy gatherings.
